Welcome to the Secure Smart Home of the Future!

These are some projects on how to create an Open Source Secure smart home.

Magic Mirror

This is for the Magic Mirror for your Secure Smart Home. You can put a mirror anywhere in the house you want easy access to your information. You could have one in the bathroom to give you a weather and calendar report for the day. You could have one in the kitchen to show grocery lists, or family calendars, you could even put one in your bedroom to look at your home energy report from the day, or even your current electrical usage and which lights are on.
