Objectives:

  • Learning how to find new information and figure out something in security you're interested in.

Complete the following problems

Pick a video from one of the following conferences, video should be from the last couple years.

  1. Def Con Safe Mode Presentations https://media.defcon.org/
  2. Diana Initiative https://www.youtube.com/c/TheDianaInitiative/videos
  3. IntroSecCon https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCx1Ektc73drCDq6W0tclZ8g (Disclaimer: I am one of the organizers for IntroSecCon and on their board)
  4. What video did you pick?
  5. Why did you pick it?
  6. Describe what your video is about in a short paragraph.
  7. Who's the presenter?
  8. What's their expertise?
  9. How did they do presenting?
  10. What's one thing you thought they did well presenting? Anything they could work on?
  11. What's one thing you learned in the video?
  12. Are you going to continue learning about this subject? Why or why not?
  13. Would you recommend this video to others? Why it why not?
  14. Write a quick review of the video including a summary of your thoughts, include the link, the presenter and any other relevant info in the discussion.
  15. Watch one video someone else recommended. Do a short write up of what you thought about the video, why, and do you agree or disagree with the review they gave?

Deliverables

Turn in a document with your answers and a link to your chosen video including title and presenter. Add your video and requested info to the discussion.
