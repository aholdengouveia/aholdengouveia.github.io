Lab Instructions

References, a video, a PowerPoint and some notes are available at my website https://www.aholdengouveia.name/IntroLinux/linuxhistory.html

Read one of the following three articles and turn in a report using the format listed below. It is very important that it is in your own words. Do not copy, plagiarize or slightly reword someone else's text.

You will be trying something called Sketchnotes. There is more information here: https://rohdesign.com/sketchnotes

For this lab you will read one of the articles and then make a page or less of notes, using the sketchnotes format, and turn in a picture of your notes. Please make sure your picture is clear. Any paper and pen is fine, but please make sure it's legible.

Article options:

  1. Linux Took Over the Web. Now, It's Taking Over the World https://www.wired.com/2016/08/linux-took-web-now-taking-world/
  2. Why Linux is the most important software project in history https://felipec.wordpress.com/2011/03/06/why-linux-is-the-most-important-software-project-in-history/
  3. Linux turns 33 in 2024 https://www.networkworld.com/article/1286507/linux-turns-33-in-2024.html"> https://www.networkworld.com/article/1286507/linux-turns-33-in-2024.html

Deliverables

Picture of your sketchnotes about the article you picked. Make sure somewhere on your notes you have your name and which article you picked.
